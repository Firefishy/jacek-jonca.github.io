ABOUT ME

I am a strategic advisor, consultant, and a data scientist. I help organizations grow and help people live happier, healthier, and more fulfilling lives. In my past life I established and lead the Graduate Center and the Office of Postdoctoral Affairs at Texas Tech University. I hold a doctoral degree in neuroimaging and a master’s degree in social psychology (small groups, organizational behavior). I am a rock climber, cyclist, (whole) foodie, a fan of diversity, and a lover of travel, adventure, and anything involving the great outdoors.
