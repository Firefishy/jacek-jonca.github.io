Division of Graduate and Postdoctoral Affairs

OPEN 24/7 & 365

The Graduate Center is a 6000-sq. ft. facility dedicated to exclusive use by Texas Tech's Graduate Students and Postdoctoral Fellows.

 

The center is located at the heart of the Lubbock campus and serves as a hub for professional and career development and graduate student and postdoctoral life activities.

 

The Graduate Center is host to a number of academic support and student services, houses a computer lab, meeting spaces, and an abundant amount of networking, leisure, and work space.

 

The facility is open to all graduate students and postdoctoral fellows 24/7 and 365 days a year with a single swipe of your Texas Tech ID.

Library Research Assistance
Walk-ins welcomed
Monday & Tuesday 2-4pm
Student Services Room, Administration 43C
and online at: http://ttu.mobi/library

Let your friendly librarian help with that daunting literature search.
  • Assistance in conducting research, locating information, and citing sources
  • Help identifying the best sources and developing effective search strategies
  • Help locating publicly available datasets
  • Help locating funding opportunities and scholarships
  • Referrals to library experts in different subject areas
  • Answers to questions about library services and policies

More information at: www.gradcenter.ttu.edu/libraries

Wreck Hunger Food Pantry
Walk-ins welcomed
Thursday noon-2pm
Student Services Room, Administration 43C

 

Our mission is to provide you and your family with food, especially during difficult times so you do not have to deal with hunger or eating unhealthy because you cannot afford healthy food. Depending on donations and current stock, we may also offer baby items, paper towels, toilet paper, toothpaste, cleaning supplies, utensils, dishes, personal hygiene items, and coupons. We provide nutrition education and develop low cost, but nutritious recipes from items in the pantry. We also connect you with other community organizations that can help you and your family beyond our scope and abilities.
 

More information at: http://www.fb.com/ttuwreckhunger/
Graduate Student Writing Center

Daily - by appointment only
Graduate Student Writing Center Suite, Administration 43

and online

Graduate students may receive help with any writing project at any stage of the writing process.

 

The GSWC offers the following services:

  • In-person writing consultations: Students may schedule one-to-one consultations with experienced tutors. These collaborative tutorials last 50 minutes. To schedule an appointment, click here.
  • Online writing consultations are available through the  University Writing Center – uwc.ttu.edu.
  • Graduate Student Writing Groups: Graduate Student Writing Groups provide structure and support for students writing theses or dissertations. Each week, these groups meet for three hours for goal setting and independent writing time. Graduate students must apply for membership each semester.

More information at: www.gradcenter.ttu.edu/writing
Professional Development
Professional Development Programming

The Graduate Center is a focal point for graduate student-oriented professional development activities. Those activities follow core competencies set for graduate students by the Division of Graduate and Postdoctoral Affairs. Those competencies aim at making graduates from Texas Tech's graduate programs more competitive in today's workforce - both inside and outside of academia.

 

A full listing of professional development programming offered by the Division of Graduate and Postdoctoral Affairs please visit http://ttu.mobi/events.

Graduate Center Facilities
The Commons

Relax. Let's face it, sometimes you just need some time to kick back and relax, maybe even take a nap! In our 2000-sq. ft. Commons, you can do all of that and more.

Networking. This shared space provides an excellent opportunity to meet graduate students from outside of your lab, program, academic department, or college. It helps build social and support networks and forge friendships that may last a lifetime.

Entertain & Study. Three 70" flat screen TVs are equipped with Netflix and YouTube so there will be no boredom in your future... except perhaps for that daunting paper that you can also write sitting in those comfy armchairs we put in the Commons.

Change. The best part is that everything is on wheels, so you can arrange the furniture just the way you like or see it fit!
Computer Lab
A fully equipped computer lab with seven PCs sporting 24" dual displays and two iMacs with 27" screens is perhaps what you have been looking for all along! All computers come with the full Texas Tech software suite, from SPSS and SAS to Adobe Creative Suite and Microsoft Office. Printing at the lab is free if you bring your own printing paper.
Study \\ Dining \\ Coffee Space
The Graduate Center offers a host of smaller spaces where you can sit and eat that lunch you just picked up at the SUB. It is also the perfect place to grab a coffee with a friend, meet fellow graduate students, and find a quiet corner where you can study or read that novel you have been promising yourself to finish for way too long!
