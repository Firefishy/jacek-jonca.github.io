Welcome to Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi

Students at Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi have the unlimited potential to change the world. By gathering knowledge and skills, and learning how to apply these to the real world, you will be supported to become a successful, global citizen.

We’ll help you get to where you want to be

Start your program online with confidence and you will have flexibility and control of your academic and career goals. You will benefit from world-class teaching from the same faculty as face to face classes. The Study Group Student Promise ensures you will have a supportive environment paired with the reassurance of our risk-free introductory period.

Why Texas A&M-Corpus Christi?

Study at the only island university in the USA. As part of the prestigious Texas A&M University System, excellent academics and ground-breaking research mean you will learn and develop your career skills in highly-rated degree programs in a multicultural learning community.

About The Island University

Live and study on the only island university in the USA, where innovation and a hands-on learning experience are at the core of your education.
